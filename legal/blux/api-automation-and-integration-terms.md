# BLUX API, Automation & Integration Terms

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

## API and Integration Access

If BLUX offers API or integration access, users must secure credentials, respect documented limits, and avoid abusive traffic patterns.

## Automation Controls

You are responsible for outputs and actions produced by your automations, agents, and downstream systems that call BLUX.

## Third-Party Dependencies

Integrations with external platforms are subject to those platforms' own terms and availability. BLUX is not responsible for third-party outages or policy changes.

## Abuse and Enforcement

BLUX may throttle, suspend, or disable integrations that create security risk, policy violations, or unacceptable operational load.
