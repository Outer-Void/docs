# BLUX / BLUX.AI Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

## Scope

This policy applies to BLUX / BLUX.AI product interfaces, AI tooling, APIs, automations, and associated support channels that reference this policy.

## Data Categories

BLUX may process account details, prompts, inputs, outputs, usage logs, integration metadata, and technical diagnostics needed for operation and security.

## Uses of Data

Data may be used to provide AI features, improve reliability, detect abuse, troubleshoot errors, enforce policy, and communicate service updates.

## Providers and Integrations

BLUX may rely on infrastructure, model, analytics, communication, and billing providers as needed for product operation.

## User Controls

Users can submit privacy and data requests through the contact methods listed on BLUX properties. Requests may be limited by legal obligations, security controls, and service integrity needs.

## Security and Retention

Reasonable safeguards may be used, but no platform can guarantee absolute security or permanent availability.
