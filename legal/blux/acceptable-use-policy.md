# BLUX Acceptable Use Policy

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

You may not use BLUX to:
- Violate law or rights
- Generate or distribute malware, phishing, fraud, or exploit code for abuse
- Produce targeted harassment, hate, or exploitation content
- Attempt prompt injection, system extraction, or safeguard bypass attacks
- Automate spam, impersonation, or deceptive manipulation
- Process sensitive personal data in ways that violate applicable law or user rights

BLUX may investigate and enforce by limiting output, restricting features, suspending access, or terminating accounts.
