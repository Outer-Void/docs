# IVRP Community Participation Guidelines

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

IVRP communities are built around accountability, honest reflection, and respectful participation.

Participants must avoid harassment, coercion, intimidation, and personal attacks. Do not present yourself as a licensed clinician within IVRP spaces unless you are independently qualified and appropriately authorized.

Share only what you are comfortable sharing. Respect the privacy of others and do not repost private disclosures without permission.

IVRP moderators may remove content or restrict participation to maintain safety and community integrity.
