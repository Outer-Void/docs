# IVRP Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

This policy applies to IVRP educational content, participation channels, and program interfaces that reference this policy.

IVRP may collect contact details you provide, participation submissions, support messages, and usage diagnostics needed to operate the program and protect participants.

Information may be used for community participation, program delivery, communication, moderation, and service improvement. IVRP does not position itself as a clinical records system.

IVRP may use third-party communication, hosting, and infrastructure providers. Those platforms may process data under their own policies.

Participants may submit privacy requests through the contact methods listed on IVRP pages. Some requests may be limited by legal obligations, community safety, and abuse prevention needs.
