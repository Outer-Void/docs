# IVRP No Medical, Clinical, or Crisis Services Disclaimer

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

IVRP does not provide medical advice, mental health diagnosis, therapy, counseling, psychiatric treatment, emergency response, or crisis intervention services.

If you may be in danger, experiencing a mental health emergency, or considering self-harm, contact local emergency services or a qualified crisis resource immediately.

IVRP is not a replacement for licensed clinicians, therapists, physicians, or emergency responders.
