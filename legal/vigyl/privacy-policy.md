# Vigyl Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

This policy applies to Vigyl accountability and support-oriented interfaces that reference this document.

Vigyl may process participant account details, check-in data, support communications, group coordination activity, and service diagnostics needed for operation and safety controls.

Data may be used to deliver accountability workflows, coordinate permitted participants, improve service quality, and prevent misuse.

Vigyl may rely on communications, hosting, and infrastructure providers. Those providers may process data within their own policy frameworks.

Users may submit privacy requests through the contact methods shown in the applicable Vigyl interface.
