# Vigyl Care Group & Shared Accountability Expectations

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

Care groups and accountability circles should establish clear expectations about communication, escalation boundaries, privacy respect, and participant consent.

Participants must avoid presenting themselves as emergency responders or licensed professionals unless they are independently qualified and acting within lawful scope.

Vigyl tools support coordination but do not replace direct communication, personal responsibility, or professional intervention when needed.
