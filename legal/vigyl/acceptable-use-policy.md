# Vigyl Acceptable Use Policy

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

Users may not use Vigyl to harass, stalk, control, intimidate, exploit, or monitor people without lawful authority and clear consent where required.

Impersonation, abusive surveillance, non-consensual tracking behavior, and coercive use of support tools are prohibited.

Vigyl may restrict or terminate accounts to protect participant safety and platform integrity.
