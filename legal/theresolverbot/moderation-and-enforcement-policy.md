# TheResolverBot Moderation & Enforcement Policy

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

TheResolverBot can support warnings, muting, filtering, role actions, and related enforcement automation where configured.

Enforcement may be based on configurable rules, heuristics, or automated detection. Results may be imperfect.

TheResolverBot maintainers may suspend or restrict bot usage where abuse, evasion, legal risk, or infrastructure harm is detected.

Community admins should define transparent local rules and appeal pathways for members.
