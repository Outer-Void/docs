# TheResolverBot Acceptable Use Policy

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

You may not use TheResolverBot to:
- Harass, profile, or target users for abuse
- Run illegal surveillance or unauthorized data collection
- Circumvent platform protections or evade moderation bans
- Deliver malware, spam, or phishing campaigns
- Abuse API behavior in ways that degrade service

Violations may result in bot removal, service denial, suspension, or permanent restrictions.
