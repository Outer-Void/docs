- [`IMPLEMENTATION-NOTES.md`](IMPLEMENTATION-NOTES.md) — Guidance for implementers and maintainers.

## Document Precedence

1. Product-specific documents govern product behavior for that product.
2. Outer-Void umbrella documents govern general ecosystem sites and services.
3. Shared documents provide baseline principles and interpretation guidance.

If there is a conflict, apply the most specific product-level document.

## Maintenance Expectations

- Update affected documents whenever a product changes its user flows, data handling, account model, moderation behavior, or monetization.
- Keep wording conservative and operationally accurate.
- Do not add compliance, certification, jurisdiction, or regulatory claims unless those claims are documented and supportable.
- Keep the **Last Updated** line current for each modified document.
