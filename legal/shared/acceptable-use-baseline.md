# Shared Acceptable Use Baseline

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

This baseline applies across Outer-Void ecosystem products unless a product-specific acceptable use policy is stricter.

## Prohibited Conduct

Users may not use any service to:
- Violate applicable law
- Harass, threaten, abuse, or exploit others
- Publish or distribute malicious software, phishing material, or credential theft tools
- Attempt unauthorized access, account takeover, scraping abuse, or platform interference
- Distribute spam, deceptive content, or fraudulent schemes
- Misuse user-generated content, private data, or confidential information
- Evade enforcement controls, bans, or technical protections

## AI and Automation Misuse

Where AI or automation is available, users may not:
- Use outputs to facilitate illegal activity or targeted abuse
- Misrepresent synthetic output as verified fact in high-risk contexts without independent review
- Attempt prompt injection or system abuse intended to bypass safeguards

## Enforcement

Violations can result in warnings, feature restrictions, suspension, termination, content removal, and reporting to relevant parties where appropriate.
