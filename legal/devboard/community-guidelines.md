# DevBoard Community Guidelines

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

DevBoard welcomes constructive technical sharing, respectful feedback, and collaborative discussion.

Do not post harassment, hate, doxxing, scams, malicious links, spam, or plagiarized work.

Critique should focus on ideas and implementation quality rather than personal attacks.

Moderation actions may include warnings, content removal, visibility limits, suspensions, or bans.
