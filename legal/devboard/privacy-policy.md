# DevBoard Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

This policy applies to DevBoard community and showcase interfaces where users browse, submit, or discuss projects.

DevBoard may process profile information, submissions, comments, moderation records, and technical usage logs required for community operations and abuse prevention.

Data may be used to publish content, operate accounts, moderate community activity, improve service reliability, and communicate about platform changes.

DevBoard may rely on hosting, analytics, communication, and moderation support providers.

Users may request privacy-related support through contact methods shown on DevBoard properties.
