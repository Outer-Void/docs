# DevBoard Intellectual Property & Takedown Guidance

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

If you believe content on DevBoard infringes your rights, submit a notice through available contact methods with enough detail to identify the claimed material and your rights basis.

DevBoard may remove, disable, or limit disputed content while review is in progress. Repeat infringing behavior may lead to account restrictions or termination.

Submitting a knowingly false claim may result in enforcement action.
