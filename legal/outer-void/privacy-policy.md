Reasonable safeguards may be applied, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure. Data may be retained as needed for operations, legal obligations, dispute resolution, and abuse prevention.

Outer-Void properties are not intended for unsupervised use by young children. Where law requires consent or supervision for minors, users and guardians are responsible for compliance.

This policy may be updated as services evolve. The most current version is identified by the Last Updated date.
