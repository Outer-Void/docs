# Outer-Void Acceptable Use Policy

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

This policy applies to Outer-Void umbrella properties and supplements product-specific acceptable use requirements.

## You Must Not

- Use Outer-Void services for unlawful conduct.
- Harass, threaten, exploit, or dox individuals or groups.
- Attempt unauthorized access, credential abuse, reverse engineering abuse, or denial-of-service behavior.
- Spread malware, phishing, fraud, deceptive spam, or harmful automation.
- Reuse content in a way that violates rights or applicable law.

## Security and Integrity

You must not test, probe, or overload infrastructure without explicit written authorization.

## Enforcement

Outer-Void may remove content, limit features, suspend access, terminate access, and preserve evidence where needed to investigate abuse and protect ecosystem integrity.
