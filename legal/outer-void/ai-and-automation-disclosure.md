# Outer-Void AI & Automation Disclosure

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

## AI and Automated Features

Some Outer-Void ecosystem products may use AI models, rules engines, or automated systems for generation, moderation support, classification, ranking, workflow assistance, or operational tooling.

## Output and Decision Limits

AI or automated outputs may be incomplete, inaccurate, outdated, or contextually wrong. Automated signals are assistive and should not be treated as guaranteed fact.

## Human Oversight

Where feasible, Outer-Void may apply human review for high-impact moderation or policy decisions, but human review is not guaranteed in every case.

## High-Risk Use Warning

Do not rely on AI or automation outputs from ecosystem tools as the sole basis for medical, legal, crisis, safety-critical, or other high-stakes decisions.
