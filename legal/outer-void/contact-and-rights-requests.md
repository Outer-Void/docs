# Outer-Void Contact & Rights Requests

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

## How to Contact

For privacy requests, policy questions, content complaints, or legal notices, use the contact methods listed on the applicable Outer-Void or product page.

## Request Categories

You may submit requests related to:
- Access to data associated with your use
- Correction requests
- Deletion requests
- Content rights and takedown concerns
- Policy interpretation and enforcement questions

## Verification and Limits

To protect users and services, identity verification may be required before sensitive actions are taken. Some requests may be denied or limited where retention is required for legal obligations, abuse prevention, security, dispute handling, or platform integrity.

## Response Expectations

Response timing may vary based on request complexity, legal constraints, and available operational capacity.
