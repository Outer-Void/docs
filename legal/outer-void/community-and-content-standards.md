# Outer-Void Community & Content Standards

**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

## Community Expectations

Outer-Void community spaces should remain constructive, respectful, and safe for participation.

## Not Allowed

- Harassment, hate, threats, stalking, or exploitation
- Doxxing, privacy invasion, or targeted abuse
- Spam, scams, deceptive promotion, or coordinated manipulation
- Illegal content or instructions for harmful activity

## User Content Responsibility

Contributors are responsible for accuracy, legality, and rights clearance for what they post.

## Moderation Discretion

Outer-Void may remove content, warn users, restrict participation, or suspend access when standards are violated or when risk to the community is identified.
