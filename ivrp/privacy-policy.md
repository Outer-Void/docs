# IVRP Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** [March 30th, 2026]

---

## 1. Overview

The Inner Void Recovery Program (IVRP) is an educational and behavioral development system designed to support individuals in self-awareness, personal accountability, and internal change.

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and protected when interacting with IVRP content, platforms, or services.

---

## 2. Information Collected

IVRP may collect the following types of information:

### a. Public Interaction Data
- Likes, comments, shares, and follows on social media platforms  
- Public profile information associated with those interactions  

### b. Direct Communication
- Messages sent via platforms such as Facebook, email, or other communication channels  

### c. Voluntary Information
- Any information voluntarily shared during discussions, participation, or inquiries  

---

## 3. How Information Is Used

Information is used strictly for:

- Responding to messages and communication  
- Supporting individuals engaging with IVRP content  
- Improving clarity, structure, and delivery of the program  
- Community interaction and engagement  

IVRP does **not sell, rent, or trade personal information**.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

Information is not shared with third parties except:

- When required by law  
- When necessary to protect safety, rights, or integrity  
- When using third-party platforms (e.g., Facebook), where their policies apply  

---

## 5. Third-Party Platforms

IVRP operates across third-party platforms including but not limited to:

- Facebook  
- Email services  
- Messaging platforms  

These platforms may collect and process data independently. Users should review their respective privacy policies.

---

## 6. Data Security

Reasonable efforts are made to protect information. However:

- No system is completely secure  
- Users should avoid sharing sensitive personal or medical information  

---

## 7. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for the information they choose to share.

IVRP encourages awareness and discretion when engaging in public or private communication.

---

## 8. Intended Use

IVRP is intended for:

- Personal development  
- Behavioral awareness  
- Recovery support (non-clinical)  

It is **not a medical, psychiatric, or licensed treatment service**.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as IVRP evolves. Updates will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date.
